View Inactive Companies

  1. Go to Quickbooks
  2. Reports -> Standard Reports
  3. Choose Sales by Customer Summary
  4. Change the dates from 13 months ago to the current month
  5. Click Compare in the top right corner, and check Previous Period, $ Change, % Change
  6. Click Export and Export to Excel in the top right corner
  7. Import here and view inactive companies
